---

**R version 4.3.3 and Matlab R2021b were used**

---

**[Data]**  
1. **Nfixer_types.xlsx** (Observed nitrogen fixation rates for different nitrogen-fixer types)  
2. **SNF.xlsx** (Symbiotic nitrogen fixation rates at the site scale)  
3. **FNF.xlsx** (Free-living nitrogen fixation rates at the site scale)  

**[Machine Learning Model]**  
1. **BNF_data.m** (Environmental data and preprocessing)  
2. **BNF_model.m** (Random forest model training and prediction)  
3. **BNF_futureRes.m** (Estimation of future biological nitrogen fixation response)
